Now the full booking flow — the most complex part of the platform. The Order Service, built with Java and Spring Boot, creates a reserved order and writes an event to the transactional outbox. The outbox scheduler publishes ORDER_PLACED to RabbitMQ. The Payment Service — also Spring Boot — consumes this, processes the payment through a simulated gateway, and publishes PAYMENT_SUCCESS back to the queue. The Order Service confirms the booking and issues the e-ticket. The seat transitions to allocated.
